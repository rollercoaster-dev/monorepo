# Roles & Permissions

## Roles

- **Mentor**: Guide badge creation, review evidence, endorse achievements
- **Peer**: Collaborate on goals, share badges, provide feedback
- **Teacher**: Set curricula, assign badges, track progress

## Permissions

- Badge creation vs endorsement vs administration
- View, edit, revoke rights per role
