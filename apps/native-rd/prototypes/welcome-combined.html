Welcome Screen Redesign — 3 Approaches

All three share a friendlier brand intro (RD logo + warmer copy). They differ in how they communicate the impact of choosing a theme — Live Mirror re-skins the whole screen, Wallpaper Gallery shows full mockups per theme, Side-by-Side compares old and new at the same time.

A · Live Mirror
Tap a chip — the entire welcome screen re-skins instantly. Smallest visual footprint for the picker; biggest "wow" when colors change.
B · Wallpaper Gallery
Each theme is a full mini-mockup styled in that theme. Like picking a phone wallpaper — you see all 7 looks at once.
C · Side-by-Side Compare
Two phone panels: "Now" and "Preview." A pill-bar swaps the preview theme. Best for comparison, requires more scrolling.
