Evidence Viewer — 3 Approaches

Tap any evidence item in any phone to open. All three approaches let you browse every piece of evidence once open.

1Bottom Sheet + Swipe Carousel
Slides up from the bottom. Swipe / arrows to browse. Light touch — keeps timeline visible behind backdrop.
2Full-screen + Thumbnail Strip
Pushes a dedicated screen. Thumbnail strip at bottom shows every item — tap to jump. Best for focused review.
3Modal + Collapsible List
Centered modal. "All evidence" list expands below to switch items. Compact, good for quick context-switching.

Click an evidence row in any phone to open that approach. Use ← / → on the carousel & full-screen, or list rows on the modal.
